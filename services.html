Our Services

What We Can Do For You

Integrated logistics Solutions for Cargo Owners

We help businesses manage their end-to-end logistics process with a practical platform that provides visibility to all its stakeholders and improves efficiency in its value chain. Leveraging on end-to-end real time data and in-depth analysis of data, we help businesses reduce supply chain risks, logistics bottlenecks, haulage risk and to improve turnaround time.

  1. Haulaa NG proactively gives you regular updates on the trip’s progress with estimated time of arrival (ETA).

  2. Haulaa NG agrees upfront with the transporters on the trip plan allowing cargo to move smoothly whilst the transporter executes the load profitably & efficiently

  3. Haulaa NG supports your warehouse or store manager with effective processes and recording, to plan manage, track and record loading, offloading, waybill, freight management, working capital management, cost control and payment

  4. Haulaa NG cargo marketplace timeously connects your demand to move cargo with vetted & verified transporters, ensuring your cargo safely reaches even the most remote destinations in the country.

Empowering Transporters and Truck Owners

Haulaa NG makes trips as efficient and profitable as possible. We help transporters get closer to cargo owners with better rates & payment terms offering attractive and profitable loads. In addition, we also help transporters with the required tools and solutions needed to manage their fleet, track their efficiency, plan their trips, and fulfill extracapacity

  1. Easily & compliantly manage fleet information in one place with detailed information about trucks, trailers, and drivers to ensure compliance of licenses, permits and travelling fees.

  2. Provides transporters with access to negotiated offers on trip pricing, payment terms, insurance, and trip financing, among others.

  3. Quicker turnaround through proactive systems and data capture leading to increased profitability.

  4. Automated and accurate invoicing through proprietary system and software.

Providing investors access to the Haulage industry

Via our Truck partnership model, we offer potential investors, an opportunity to Own or Co-own trucks of their choice to enable them earn good return on investment throughout the duration of the partnership. We also welcome Partnerships from those who already own any vehicle that meets our specification in return for higher return on every trip completed with their asset. We lose millions of naira every week due to low supply of vehicles required to meet the high demand for our service. Thus, we have decided to partner with smart investors who are willing to leverage our experience and expertise through truck investment and asset finance Partnership. We buy trucks based on logistics contract requirement.

  1. We procure the truck and carry out proper profiling on the truck from registration through comprehensive insurance, tracking, among others.

  2. We handle the vehicle operations from driver recruitment, vehicle maintenance, and excellent customer service to our clients.

  3. Your duty as a partner is to sign up and put your investment to work.

  4. You earn highly competitive return on investment based on the project you have signed up for and you decide how your returns are to be paid while your initial capital or asset is returned at maturity of your investment plan.

We recognize the significance of approaching each task with utmost diligence and dedication.
