TRUCK PARTNERSHIP PLAN

Do you own a 10 Ton or 15 Ton Truck in good condition and you will like to make more money off it? Haulaa NG can take the load of you by helping you to run and manage it while you earn income monthly.

  1. Your investment is fully secured with your truck. Thus, the risk of loss of capital is zero as the truck is also fully insured to cover for theft, accident, or any risk.
  2. You have the option to sell the truck to us at any time. A 3-month notice period is required.
  3. You do not need to source for business for the truck as Haulaa NG already has firm haulage contracts with a number of medium to large-scale manufacturers, wholesalers, distributors, and retailers.

Scope

  1. Every truck registered under Haulaa NG will be valued and assessed.
  2. The valued truck must be in good condition with complete accessories.
  3. Haulaa NG will cover the cost of fuel, driver, and motor boy.
  4. Drivers and Motor boys will be sourced through Haulaa NG. However, if the truck already has a driver, Haulaa NG will register and work with the existing driver.
  5. Haulaa NG will be liable for maintenance up to 100,000 monthly.

Terms and Conditions

  1. Truck must be insured.
  2. All documents must be up to date which includes midyear permit.
  3. Driver must possess a valid driver’s license and must have at least 5 years driving experience.
  4. Truck must be roadworthy and possess all the required truck accessories.
  5. Truck must have GIT (Goods in Transit) insurance.
